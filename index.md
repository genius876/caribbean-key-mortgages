Our Process

## How does it all work?

Our mortgage process is a simple 4 step process to make sure you get all you need, in the simplest and most stress-free way possible.

### Initial Assessment & Intake

We review your income, goals, and documents to calculate a realistic mortgage estimate based on our lender requirements.

### Conditional Approval & Requirements

The bank reviews your file and issues conditional approval. We guide you through fulfilling conditions such as insurance, appraisal, and proof of funds.

### Application & Lender Selection

We help you choose the right lender, prepare your documents, and submit your mortgage application.

### Final Approval & Closing

Once conditions are met, the bank issues final approval. The notary prepares the deed, and you complete the property transfer.
